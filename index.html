"Hello there,"

My name is Alex Pottebaum, a former Animal Keeper turned UX/UI Designer who makes innovative, accessible, and user-focused creations. I enjoy working on all aspects of the design process but I specialize in User Empathy, Information Organization, and building Low to High Fidelity prototypes on Figma.

"Film Festival Rebuild"

The original Orlando Film Festival (O.F.F.) website had numerous accessibility, aesthetic, and architectural choices that were not ideal. Our group rebuilt the site from the ground up and even created a website that highlighted the festivals qualities.

"DOI Redesign"

While the original Department of the Interior (DOI) website was aesthetically pleasing, it was missing key details about what it actually does. This redesign is focused around helping users understand what the DOI actually does while making the aesthetic feel more unique.

"Connect"

"Connect” is a travel-app designed to make planning and traveling with others. Using joint-planning, shared information, and meetups “Connect” helps you make your dream trips easier.
